Frequently Asked Questions

How is Fundly different from other saving apps?

Fundly combines traditional group saving practices with modern technology. Unlike individual saving apps, we focus on collective accountability and transparent group decision-making for withdrawals.

Is my money safe with Fundly?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and partner with regulated financial institutions. Your funds are FDIC insured and we never access your money without group consensus.

What if I need emergency access to funds?

Groups can vote on emergency withdrawals. We understand student life is unpredictable, so we've built flexibility into our voting system for urgent situations.
